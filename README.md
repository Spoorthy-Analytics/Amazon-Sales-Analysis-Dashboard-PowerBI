# Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard | Power BI
## Project Overview
This Power BI project provides a comprehensive sales analysis dashboard for Amazon products. The dashboard helps business stakeholders monitor sales performance, product categories, customer reviews, and sales trends across different time periods.
---
## Dashboard Highlights
### KPI Metrics
- YTD Sales: $2.18M
- QTD Sales: $811.09K
- YTD Products Sold: 27.75K
- YTD Reviews: 19.42M
### Business Insights
- Sales by Month
- Sales by Week
- Product Category Performance
- Top 5 Products by Sales
- Top 5 Products by Reviews
- Interactive Filters
---

## Dashboard Features
### Sales Trend Analysis
Track monthly and weekly sales performance.
### Product Category Analysis
Compare category-wise sales contribution.
### Product Performance Tracking
Identify top-selling products and products with the highest customer engagement.
### Interactive Filters
- Product Category
- Quarter Selection
---
## Key Business Questions Answered
1. What is the total YTD Sales?
2. Which product category generates the highest revenue?
3. Which products contribute most to sales?
4. How do sales vary across months and weeks?
5. Which products receive the most customer reviews?
